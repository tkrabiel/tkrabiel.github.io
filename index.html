NSPS-THSOA Hydrographer Certification Portal

This site is designed to streamline the application process for professional hydrographer certifications. The U.S. Hydrographer Certification program, administered by the NSPS-THSOA Hydrographer Board, provides professional recognition for hydrographers who meet established education, training, and experience standards.

Available Certification Levels:

  • Level 1 - Certified Master Hydrographer (CMH)
  • Level 2 - Certified Hydrographer (CH)

Both levels require documentation of education, training, and experience, as well as ongoing professional development.

For more information go to the NSPS Certification Website
Important Data Notice: This portal does not store your information on a server. You will be saving your data as a .json file. You are responsible for managing this file. After filling out your application or when you wish to save your progress, export the Master JSON file to your computer. You must upload this file next time to continue working.
